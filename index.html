Pagero Universal Format (PUF)

A set of specifications based on Universal Business Language (UBL) that enables the exchange of compliant business documents worldwide via our e-business platform Pagero Online.

Specifications

PUF Billing 2.0
For sending tax compliant invoices and credit notes worldwide. PUF Technical Acknowledgement 1.0
For receiving message level response. PUF Clearance Notification 1.0
For receiving response from clearance authorities. PUF Invoice Response 1.0
For sending and/or receiving business level response from buyers.
